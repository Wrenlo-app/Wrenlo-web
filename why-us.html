What about Jobber Receptionist or Housecall Pro's AI features?

FSM-native receptionist features are designed to work within their own platform. They handle basic call answering for existing customers but don't offer missed-call text-back to callers who hung up, trade-specific emergency classification, revenue attribution by lead source, or quote follow-up sequences. Common objections

Things contractors ask us
before they start.

"My customers want to talk to a real person."
So do we. Wrenlo introduces itself honestly as the team's AI assistant — never pretends to be human. Most homeowners (53% by one industry study) are comfortable with AI on an initial call, especially when the alternative is voicemail. For customers who want a human, Wrenlo escalates to the owner or can be set to transfer immediately. The Pro plan includes a human backup option for calls where AI isn't appropriate.
"I already pay for Jobber/HCP — why add another tool?"
Jobber and Housecall Pro are excellent at running jobs you already have. Wrenlo captures the jobs that would otherwise never make it into your FSM — the calls you miss while on a job, the after-hours leads, the callers who don't leave voicemail. It feeds new jobs into your existing FSM rather than replacing it. Most contractors see positive ROI in the first week of the pilot.
"What if Wrenlo says something wrong to a customer?"
Wrenlo uses constrained, pre-approved scripts — it doesn't improvise. Emergency calls escalate to the owner without autonomous troubleshooting. Safety-critical situations (gas smell, electrical sparking, structural risk) immediately route to the owner without Wrenlo attempting a response. Every call has a full transcript and audit trail in your lead inbox. You can review, edit the playbook, and tighten rules at any time.
"I don't want to change my phone number."
You don't. Call forwarding redirects missed and after-hours calls to Wrenlo from your existing number — at the carrier or Google Voice level. Your customers still call the same number they always have. You can set forwarding rules: after 3 rings, after 5pm, or only on weekends. It takes about 5 minutes to configure and can be turned off instantly.
"I'm already pretty good at calling back fast."
Great — but what happens at 9pm on a Tuesday when you're at your kid's game? Or when you're under a crawlspace for 3 hours on a Friday? Wrenlo covers the gaps that even the most responsive owner can't fill. It also handles the 30–40% of callers who hang up before leaving voicemail — people you never even knew called.
"$399 a month feels expensive for a small business."
One recovered HVAC repair at $900 covers 2+ months of the Growth plan. One recovered replacement estimate covers the year. The ROI calculator on our pricing page lets you run your own numbers based on your actual call volume and average ticket. Most pilots in our founding cohort recover their first month's cost within the first week. If Wrenlo doesn't recover a qualified opportunity during the 14-day pilot, you don't continue.
