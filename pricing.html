Everything you want to know before spending a dollar.

The setup fee covers configuring your call forwarding, building your trade-specific intake script (including HVAC system age, model, warranty fields), setting your service area and booking rules, connecting your calendar, configuring owner escalation, and running a live test call before you go live. Most customers are fully configured in under 2 hours. The fee is credited in full toward your first month's subscription — so your first invoice reflects the plan cost only.
The Growth setup ($699) includes everything in Starter setup plus configuring all trade playbooks, Zapier or webhook integration, multi-trade intake, owner approval mode, emergency escalation rules, and a 60-minute live walkthrough with your team. It's flat — no surprise range. If your setup genuinely requires custom CRM workflow or API work beyond our standard stack, we'll scope that separately and tell you upfront before charging anything additional.
After every call, Wrenlo tags it as one of five categories: Urgent (no AC, no heat, active leak, safety concern), Maintenance (routine service, filter change, inspection), SPAM (robocall, scam, misdial), Vendor (supplier, sales rep, parts call), or Other (general inquiry, pricing questions). This means your lead inbox surfaces what actually needs action — not a wall of unread transcripts.
Immediately after a call results in a booked appointment, Wrenlo sends the homeowner a confirmation SMS with the date, time window, and your business name. Thirty minutes before the scheduled visit, a second SMS goes out reminding them to be available — and offers a simple reply option to request a different time if needed. This runs automatically with no manual steps from you or your team.
Never. We notify you before you approach your plan's sizing — no surprise shutoffs. You can upgrade mid-month or add a usage bundle. For HVAC businesses during peak summer or a storm surge, we offer seasonal call bundles (300 extra calls for a flat add-on fee). We never cut off service without warning, and we never bill per-call overages without your knowledge.
Full Growth plan access for 14 days: 24/7 AI call answering, missed-call text-back within 30 seconds, smart call classification, trade-specific intake, emergency classification, calendar booking, owner escalation, booking confirmation SMS, pre-visit reminders, and daily lead summaries. At the end of the pilot, we send you a full ROI report showing calls handled, leads qualified, estimates booked, and revenue recovered.
No contract on monthly billing. Cancel anytime — your service runs until the end of the paid period. Annual plans are non-refundable after 30 days but can be paused for seasonal businesses.
Setup fees and subscriptions are collected via Stripe invoice or payment link — sent after your pilot call. We don't have a self-serve checkout yet. This keeps onboarding hands-on so you're never left configuring things alone.
