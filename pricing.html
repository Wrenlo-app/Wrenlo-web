The setup fee covers configuring your call forwarding, building your trade-specific intake script, setting up your service area and booking rules, connecting your calendar, configuring owner escalation, and running a live test call before you go live. Most customers are fully configured in under 2 hours. The fee is credited in full toward your first month's subscription.
We notify you before you hit your cap — no surprise overages. You can upgrade your plan mid-month or purchase a usage bundle. We never cut off service without warning. Usage limits exist to protect call quality, not to create billing surprises.
